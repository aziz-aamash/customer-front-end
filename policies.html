💰 Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Vision Giants, we stand behind our products with a 30-day hassle-free return and refund guarantee.

Return Eligibility

Products are eligible for return if:

  • Return request is submitted within 30 days of delivery
  • Product is unused and in original, unopened packaging
  • All accessories and documentation are included
  • Product has no signs of tampering or damage from customer misuse
  • Original receipt or order confirmation is provided

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support at support@visiongiants.pk
  2. Provide order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and shipping label
  4. Pack product securely and ship to us
  5. Product will be inspected upon receipt

Refund Timeline

  • Inspection: 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
  • Processing: 3-5 business days after approval
  • Bank Transfer: 2-3 business days

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are NOT eligible for refund:

  • Customized or made-to-order products
  • Digital products or software (once downloaded)
  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling
  • Opened or used electronics (unless defective)
  • Items purchased during final clearance sales

Defective Products

If you receive a defective product, we will replace it free of charge or issue a full refund, regardless of the 30-day window. Contact support with photos of the defect.

Shipping Costs

  • We cover return shipping for defective items
  • Customer covers return shipping for non-defective returns (unless manufacturer error)
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable

Exchanges

Need a different size, color, or model? Exchanges are free within 30 days. Simply contact support and we'll process your exchange at no additional cost.

📦 Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

We ship to all major cities across Pakistan with fast, reliable, and tracked delivery.

Shipping Partners

  • TCS (Tranzum): Coverage to all cities, 2-5 business days
  • FedEx: Express delivery for urgent orders, 1-3 business days
  • Local Courier: Same-day/next-day delivery in Multan and nearby areas

Shipping Rates

  • Free Shipping: Orders over Rs 25,000
  • Standard Shipping: Rs 250 for orders under Rs 25,000
  • Express Shipping: Rs 500 (1-2 business days, Karachi/Lahore)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Rs 700 (Multan only, orders before 12 PM)

Delivery Timeline

  • Processing: 1-2 business days (excludes weekends/holidays)
  • Shipping: 2-5 business days (standard) or 1-2 business days (express)
  • Total: 3-7 business days for standard delivery

Order Tracking

All orders receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours of dispatch. Track your package in real-time using TCS/FedEx tracking systems or contact us for updates.

Remote Areas

Shipping to remote or mountainous areas may take 7-10 business days. Additional shipping charges may apply. Please provide accurate contact information.

Shipping Address

Please ensure your address includes:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Complete street address
  • City and postal code
  • Any special delivery instructions

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your package arrives damaged:

  • Do NOT refuse the package; accept it and document the damage
  • Take photos of the packaging and damaged contents
  • Contact us within 48 hours with photos and order number
  • We will file a claim with the courier and send a replacement at no cost

Lost Shipments

If your package doesn't arrive within the promised timeframe:

  • Check tracking status for updates
  • Wait an additional 3-5 business days
  • Contact us with order details; we'll file a claim
  • Once claim is approved, we'll send a replacement or issue a full refund

International Shipping

Currently, Vision Giants ships only within Pakistan. International orders are not available at this time. We're working on expanding our reach!

✅ Authenticity Guarantee

Last Updated: January 2025

At Vision Giants, authenticity is our promise. Every product is 100% original and sourced directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers.

Our Commitment

We guarantee that every product sold on Vision Giants is:

  • ✓ 100% Authentic and Original
  • ✓ Never counterfeit, refurbished, or used
  • ✓ In original, factory-sealed packaging
  • ✓ Backed by full manufacturer warranty
  • ✓ Sourced from authorized distributors only

Why You Can Trust Us

  • Direct Supplier Relationships: We work directly with manufacturers and authorized distributors, eliminating middlemen and counterfeit risks
  • Rigorous Quality Checks: Every product undergoes inspection before shipping
  • Transparent Sourcing: We provide batch/serial number verification for high-value items
  • Warranty Support: Full manufacturer warranty honored on all products
  • Industry Certifications: Vision Giants is verified and certified by relevant electronics authorities

How We Verify Authenticity

  1. Supplier verification: All suppliers are audited and verified
  2. Product inspection: Physical inspection of packaging and build quality
  3. Serial number validation: Cross-reference with manufacturer databases
  4. Hologram/security feature check: Verification of anti-counterfeiting measures
  5. Documentation review: Inspection of warranty cards and certificates

Suspect a Counterfeit?

If you believe a product is not authentic:

  • Contact us immediately with order number and photos
  • Do not open or use the product
  • We will arrange immediate pickup and full refund
  • We'll also investigate the supplier and report to authorities if needed

Authenticity Guarantee Promise

If we cannot verify authenticity within 7 days:

  • Full refund + Rs 1,000 compensation
  • Free return shipping covered by Vision Giants
  • Lifetime discount on next purchase

Manufacturer Warranty

All Vision Giants products include full manufacturer warranty. We handle all warranty claims on your behalf:

  • 1-Year Standard Warranty: Covers manufacturing defects
  • Extended Warranty: Available for purchase (optional)
  • On-Site Support: Available for select products

Product Documentation

Every product includes:

  • Original manufacturer box and packaging
  • Official warranty card with hologram
  • User manual and technical documentation
  • Authenticity certificate (for high-value items)
